Nervous System Regulation is Non-Negotiable
This program is built on the principle that your body cannot adapt to training if it is stuck in a stress response. Before you add weight, reps, or volume, you have to prove you can regulate.
Nasal breathing between exercises — no mouth breathing unless you are mid-rep
Complete only what is programmed — no spontaneous cardio, no extra reps
5 minutes of downregulation before bed — legs elevated, slow exhales, no phone
Anchor protein at two meals minimum — your body needs fuel to stabilize

Phase 1 — Observe
Day 1
Movement is information.

Today is not about performance. It is about gathering data. Your body will show you where it compensates, where it locks, and where it lacks control. Pay attention.

2 Minute Nasal Breathing Reset
Inhale 4 seconds. Exhale 6 seconds. Move fluidly. No rushing between exercises.

Day 4
